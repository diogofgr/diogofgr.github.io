NewGame+

This is a web app for buying and selling your used console games. As a seller you can list your game by searching through all the games ever made (using an API). As a buyer you can see all the games other users are selling, grouped by game title (i.e. if you search for Vice City you will get only one result with a list of sellers bellow).

Work in progress.

R.U.O.K.

This web app does Sentiment Analisys using Artificial Inteligence. It's meant for managers, CEO's, professors, etc (people who manage teams) and it basically sends questionnaires, analizes if people are sad or happy, and gives you charts. You get notified if there's someone in your team that's feeling down.
